Our philosophy
The land heals.

We simply get out of the way.

Our open-air spa is a refuge built on a single belief: that the body knows how to heal, and that nature knows how to help. Ancient Mayan wisdom — cacao, copal, cenote water, obsidian — woven into rituals designed to bring you back to yourself.

Every treatment is performed by certified Mayan healers and wellness practitioners who were born here, who understand this land, and who carry traditions passed across generations.

The spa experience

What to expect when you arrive

Your spa day at CHI*KA unfolds at its own pace. This is not a schedule — it's a rhythm.

i

Arrival & Intention Setting

You're welcomed with a warm herbal infusion and a quiet intake conversation with your healer — no intake forms, just a conversation about what you're carrying and what you'd like to release.

ii

Cleansing Ritual

A copal smoke cleansing opens the session, clearing energy and preparing the body to receive. Ancient. Quiet. Immediate.

iii

Your Treatment

Whether a deep-tissue massage, a cacao ceremony, or our signature cenote ritual — performed with intention, presence, and an hour free from time.

iv

Integration & Rest

Post-treatment, a private hammock space is yours for as long as you need. Herbal tea, silence, and the sound of the jungle doing its work.
