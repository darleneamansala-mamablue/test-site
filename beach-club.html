The philosophy
Not a facility.

CHI*KA's beach club
is a philosophy.

There are no pool chairs lined up in formation. No reserved towel system. Just palapas spaced wide enough for actual privacy, a bar that makes things properly, and a kitchen that sources everything from within a hundred kilometres.

We're open to hotel guests and outside visitors — though if the vibe finds you, you might book a room before the day is done.
